Frequently Asked Questions

How much does this service cost?

Our pricing varies based on the scope of your project. We offer flexible packages and project-based pricing. We'll discuss costs during your free initial consultation.

How long does a typical move take?

It depends on your situation. Some moves take a few weeks, while others may take a few months. We'll create a timeline that works for you during our planning phase.

Can you help even if we're not sure about moving?

Absolutely! We can help you explore your options and make the decision that's right for you. Many clients start with a consultation to gather information.

What about selling my current home?

While we don't handle real estate sales, we can help prepare your home for sale and coordinate the timeline with your move.

Can you handle large estates?

Yes! We have experience with homes of all sizes and complexities. We'll coordinate everything needed for your specific situation.

What if I have items of sentimental value?

We treat every item with respect and understand that some belongings have deep emotional value. We'll work with you thoughtfully to honor what matters most.
