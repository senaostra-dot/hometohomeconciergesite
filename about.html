About Sena

Get to know the person behind Right Size with Sena

Who is Sena?

Sena is a compassionate, detail-oriented Senior Move & Right-Sizing Specialist with a deep passion for helping older adults navigate one of life's biggest transitions. With years of experience in property management, community development, and working directly with seniors, Sena has found her true calling in making moves meaningful and stress-free.

Her Approach

Sena brings warmth, professionalism, and genuine care to every project. She believes that downsizing isn't about getting rid of things—it's about creating space for what truly matters. Her thoughtful process honors the memories attached to a lifetime of belongings while helping you envision your next chapter with excitement and possibility.

What Drives Her

Sena is driven by the relationships she builds with her clients. She listens carefully to understand your priorities, your concerns, and your dreams for your new home. Whether you're moving to a smaller space, relocating to a senior community, or simply reimagining your current home, Sena treats your move with the personal attention and respect it deserves.

Beyond the Move

Outside of her professional work, Sena is a passionate volunteer and community member who loves bringing people together. She enjoys spending time with family, exploring nature, and finding creative ways to serve others. Her enthusiasm and genuine heart for people make her an invaluable partner in your next chapter.
